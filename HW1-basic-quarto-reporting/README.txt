# Spotify Music Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes a dataset of 948 popular Spotify songs, examining relationships between musical characteristics (energy, danceability, tempo, mode) and streaming performance.

## Key Findings
- Studio recordings significantly outperform live recordings in streaming numbers
- Major and minor mode songs show similar streaming success despite differences in danceability
- Musical success is multifaceted and not determined by individual attributes alone

## Analysis Components
- **Visualization**: Comparison of live vs. studio recordings across key metrics
- **Summary Table**: Statistical comparison of major vs. minor mode songs
- **Interactive Table**: Exploration of all songs by streaming performance

## Dependencies
- Packages: tidyverse, knitr, kableExtra, DT
